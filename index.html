Sound of Snow Falling

White plummets from the heavens, feathers
Plucked from the wings of angels
By a frost-born giant
Grinning through his blood stained teeth.
The fallen angels are whisked away by the sin
Of humanity, their bodies bound to earthly trifles,
Bereft of redemption.

I sit divided,
Only an onlooker
To this violent storm.
The thin glass between cruelty and I trembles in
Its wake.
